About Us
Vision
Engineering Excellence,
Delivered Efficiently.
Collaborated with a group of highly motivated professionals having vast, rich, and worthy experience across the iron and steel plants within India and around the world, we are poised to provide an overall optimal engineered solution to customers well within their budget and time schedule. Our location at Gangavathi and Bhubaneswar are chosen wisely to remain in close proximity to the customers, to ensure customer delight in servicing plant and units in the most cost-effective and most productive manner.
Mission
Innovating for a Self-Reliant
Steel Industry.
Our mission is to develop a thriving and sustainable business model to substitute expensive spares and expat services to the Indian iron and steel industries continually and effectively. We intend to become a leading spares manufacturer, spares aggregator, and services provider for the equipment in the iron and steel industry and serve as an alternative to OEM.

Engineering Excellence for a Stronger Future

Moved by the Idea of Atma-Nirbhar (Self Reliant ) Bharat initiative from the Govt. of India , a group of Highly motivated Technocrats having vast , very rich and worthy experience across the Iron and steel plants within India and around the world, came together to kick start a new business model substituting expensive spares and expat services for the Indian Steel Industries. The approach is in sync with the “Atma-Nirbhar Bharat ” initiative and our location Karnataka,Odisha & West Bengal, India are in close proximity to most of the customer , providing us a great opportunity to mitigate the pain issues of our esteemed customers.
